# DIA-002B: The forces, not balanced
## Snapshot
- **Diagram file:** `garden/content/ai-governance/part-1/diagrams/DIA-002B-capital-ring.svg`
- **Theme:** Net-force view of AI capital momentum
- **Primary audience takeaway:** Brake forces are present, but continuation forces are drawn as larger and faster, so trajectory still leans forward.

## What The Diagram Shows
The visual borrows a free-body force layout around `THE STRUCTURE`, the AI capital web. On the left, three thick rust arrows push forward: operational profit, race pressure, and exit-cost asymmetry. On the right, three thinner green arrows resist: delayed profitability discipline, antitrust regulation, and public scrutiny. A larger net arrow below points right, turning the central claim into geometry: brakes exist, but they do not match the forward load.

## Elements And Flow
- **Components:**
  - Central object: "THE STRUCTURE" / AI capital web
  - Continuation-force column (R1 operational profit, R3 race pressure, exit cost > continuation cost)
  - Brake-force column (B1 profitability discipline, antitrust/regulation, public scrutiny)
  - Bottom net-force indicator and concluding caption
- **Relationships:**
  - Each left-side force is paired against a right-side counterforce on the same horizontal level
  - Forward arrows are visibly thicker/longer than backward arrows to encode relative strength
  - The bottom arrow aggregates all paired interactions into a single directional result
- **Notable labels or metrics:**
  - "$600B+ committed"
  - "verifies on a 5-7 year delay"
  - "exit cost > continuation cost"
  - "the brakes are not fictional - they are simply outweighed"
  - "They run slower than the structure they oppose."

## How To Read It
1. Use the column headers to separate continuation from braking forces.
2. Read each horizontal pair as one contest, from top pair to bottom pair.
3. Compare thickness and arrow length because visual mass carries the argument.
4. Keep the center box in view to track what all forces act on.
5. Finish at the bottom net-force arrow for the aggregate direction.

## Governance Relevance
This framing helps governance distinguish existence from effectiveness. A brake can be real yet weak, late, or hard to enforce against sunk commitments and competitive signaling. That is the practical warning in the diagram. Measures that move slowly may still matter symbolically while failing to redirect the underlying capital motion.

## Suggested Caption
DIA-002B renders AI capital as an imbalanced force system where visible brakes are outweighed by stronger, quicker continuation pressures.

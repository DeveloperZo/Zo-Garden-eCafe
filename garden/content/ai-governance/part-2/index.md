Twelve places to push the system — ranked by power, not visibility. Most "AI safety" activity clusters at the least effective tier. This guide maps where effort actually produces structural change, and who is best positioned to apply it.

---

This guide takes the twelve leverage points from [[../part-1/index|Part 1's]] structural analysis and makes them actionable. Four sections come first — they map where you already sit in the system, what different kinds of action actually move, what historical precedent shows has worked, and how to read your own access to the tiers. Then the leverage tier cards.

> [!tip] Tiers aren't exclusive
> Work in multiple tiers simultaneously. Visibility work (High Leverage) builds the evidence base that makes Coordination (Transformative) possible. Liability cases (High/Medium Leverage) change what company legal teams tell product teams without waiting for legislation. These levers interact — progress on one opens space on others.
